My name is Satya Gopal. My pronouns are they/he. I’m a psychologist based out of Bengaluru, India, and I run a private practice under the name Queer Health.

Who I work with

I’m currently available for psychotherapy sessions with Individuals, Couples, and Polycules. I work with Individuals from the age of 11 to 45 years old, and am fluent in Hindi, Tamil, and English.

My specialisations include Gender, Sex and Sexuality, Kinks and Fetishes, Consensual Non-Monogamy and Polyamory, Addiction, Trauma, Relationships, Stress, and Low Mood.

Availability

I work Tuesdays through Saturdays between 10 am and 6 pm IST. Each session lasts 50 minutes. Online sessions are on Google Meet and in-person sessions are available on request in Rajaji Nagar or Malleshwaram Area, Bengaluru.

Training

After graduating with a master’s degree in Clinical Psychology, I trained intensively in Gender and Sexual Health Counselling from organisations like The Alternative Story, Bengaluru, JSS Medical College, Mysore and Heart It Out, Bengaluru.

Approaches to Therapy

My approach to therapy is queer- and trans-affirmative from the ground-up. I primarily work in the field of Gender and Sexual Health, and I use a trauma informed approach. Some therapuetic models that I derive from are: Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT) and Internal Family Systems (IFS) models as well.

Offerings

Apart from doing psychotherapy, I often offer training to therapists, professors, college students, and organisations on Consent, Boundaries, Pleasure, Masturbation, Sex Toys, and Queer and Trans inclusivity. I also offer training to therapists looking to conduct queer- and trans-affirmative counselling.

My goal is to create an inclusive and affirmative space for trans individuals in South India through therapy, training, and policy changes.
