Hey.

My name is Satya Gopal (they/he). I’m a psychologist based out of Bengaluru, India. Queer Health is the name of my psychotherapy practice.

I’m currently available for psychotherapy sessions with Individuals, Couples and Polycules. I work with Individuals from the age of 18 to 45 years old. I speak Hindi, Tamil and English.

I work from Tuesdays to Saturdays between 10 am to 6 pm IST (Friday, In person slots in Bangalore). Each session is of 50 minutes. Online sessions are on Google Meet and in-person sessions in Bengaluru are in JP Nagar 7th Phase.
